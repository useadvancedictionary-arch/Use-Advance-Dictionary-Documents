USE Advance Dictionary — account and data deletion

This page is published for Google Play and users who need to request deletion of an app-related account or associated data held by the developer.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to useadvancedictionary@gmail.com.
  2. Use the same email address as your in-app administrator account if you signed in with email and password.
  3. Describe what you want removed (for example: Firebase Authentication identifiers, cloud dictionary data associated with your use of optional sync, or all developer-held data tied to your request).

What may be deleted or retained

  • Developer-controlled cloud data (for example, data stored in the developer’s Firebase / Firestore project for optional sync) may be deleted or anonymised where technically feasible after we verify your request.
  • Data that stays only on your device (for example, local dictionary files, history, or favorites) is not removed by us remotely; you can clear it with Clear storage for the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.
  • Anonymous sign-in used only for optional sync may not include a traditional “account” you can log into; include any details shown in the app (for example, that you use cloud sync) so we can interpret your request.
